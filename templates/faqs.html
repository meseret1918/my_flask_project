General Questions

What is SoftwareCareerLink?
SoftwareCareerLink is a platform designed to help software engineers connect with career opportunities in the tech industry.
How do I sign up?
You can sign up by clicking on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage and filling out the registration form.
What courses are offered?
We offer a range of courses from introductory programming to advanced data science and machine learning.
How do I access my courses?
Once you've signed up and logged in, you can access your courses from the "My Courses" section of your dashboard.
